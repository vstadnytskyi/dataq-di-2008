set_sampling_rate(rate=200, baserate=200, dec=0, echo=True)[source]¶

Integer ranges for both variable are: 4 ≤ srate ≤ 2232 1 ≤ dec ≤ 32767 The formula to calculate sample throughput rate differs by the number of enabled channels. For a single enabled analog channel: Sample rate throughput (Hz) = 8,000 ÷ (srate × dec) resulting in a sample throughput range of 2000 Hz at its fastest, and 0.000109 Hz, or 1 sample every 9141.99 seconds. The formula changes when two or more analog channels are enabled: Sample rate throughput (Hz) = 800 ÷ (srate × dec) resulting in a sample throughput range of 200 Hz at its fastest, and 0.000011 Hz, or 1 sample every 91419.93 seconds.
